How to Play

Welcome to Blackjack! Follow these steps:

  1. Enter your bet amount.
  2. Click "Deal" to start the game.
  3. Click "Hit" to request another card.
  4. Click "Stand" to keep your current hand.
  5. Click "Double Down" to double your bet and receive one more card.
